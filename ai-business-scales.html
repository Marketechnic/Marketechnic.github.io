Free AI Business Scales

Welcome to our Free AI Business Scales Page!

As a consultant, professor, and entrepreneur, I understand the high costs of accessing validated research scales & surveys to gain insights into your organization.

That’s why I created this free resource using Chat GPT—to make it easy for anyone to explore the different scales/surveys that researchers use to assess key areas of business performance and organizational health.

Below, you’ll find a collection of valuable, research-backed scales to help you understand and enhance your organization.

If you have any additional scales you want included, please don’t hesitate to contact me at contact@marketechnic.com!

  • Burnout Risk Scale: Measures emotional exhaustion, depersonalization, and reduced personal accomplishment.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility Scale: Assesses Environmental, Social, and Economic Responsibility.
  • Employee Engagement Scale: Captures Emotional Commitment, Job Satisfaction, and more.
  • Entrepreneurial Orientation Scale: Insights into Innovativeness, Risk-Taking, and Proactiveness.
  • Familiness Scale: Explores Human Capital, Social Capital, and Governance in family businesses.

  • Leadership Style Scale: Identifies Autocratic, Democratic, and Transformational preferences.
  • Market Orientation Scale: Focuses on Customer Orientation, Competitor Orientation, and more.
  • Organizational Commitment Scale: Assesses Affective, Continuance, and Normative Commitment.
  • Organizational Culture Scale: Explores Innovation, Risk-Taking, and Team Orientation.
  • Perceived Organizational Support Scale: Measures Recognition, Support, and Fair Treatment.
  • Personality Types Scale: Based on the Five Factor Model (Big Five Traits).
